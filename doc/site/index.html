Behavioral Analysis Through Innovative Architecture and Design

Burst excels at measuring, organizing, and pattern matching trends and causes for behaviors. While traditional database solutions have been used to analyze behaviors, their original focus on relationships instead of event histories means they fail in performance when used at scale. Burst solves the behavior analysis problem through innovative architecture and design.

Burst Use Cases

Flurry Analytics, the world's most adopted mobile app analytics platform, uses Burst to power its query tool, Explorer. Flurry Analytics' customers are able through Burst's technology, to run on demand queries to gain insights into their users' in app behavior. Queries can be easily modified with filters to uncover actionable insights that ultimately lead to better mobile applications.
